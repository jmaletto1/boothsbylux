Make the most of both services in one compact package!

Roaming Photo Booth Hire

So you’ve seen Photo Booths, seen Magic Mirrors, but have you seen the hottest new trend of 2021? Introducing the iRoamer! This is essentially a roaming photo booth that comes to your guests. This means you can throw shapes on the dance floor, at the bar, or even on the terrace outside!

The Roaming Booth is the perfect combination of a Photo Booth and a Photographer. If you want a service that provides the best of both worlds, this is it! The camera itself is a lightweight, portable unit that our Booth Specialist can happily hold for 3-4 hours. This service is particularly popular among clients who want a slightly different sort of photo entertainment at the start of the night to the end. For instance, consider having the iRoamer at the start of the night whilst guests are attending the drinks reception, and then move over to a 360 Photo Booth later in the evening.

The history of the Roamer has been explored by Photobooth.net. Also consider checking out our 360 Photo Booth and Magic Mirrors. Get in touch with us to see how the Roaming Photo Booth can enhance your event!

Roamer Highlights:

TOP-NOTCH SERVICE

The Roaming Photo Booth comes for a minimum of 2 hours and is run by one of our highly presentable, super sociable and smartly dressed booth butlers! The butler will roam the venue, meaning you can take pictures at the Drinks Reception, Bar or even on the Dance floor!

SOCIAL MEDIA UPLOADS

The Roaming Booth (or iRoamer) can be booked with social media uploads (meaning guests can upload to their socials straight after taking a photo) or can be booked with a separate print station for 4×6 prints.

WEDDING GUESTBOOKS

Guestbooks are the perfect addition for Weddings and Birthdays. When your guests finish taking a photo, our Booth Specialist will print an additional copy for the Guestbook. In it, guests will be encouraged to leave a message next to their photo.

ONLINE GALLERY

Our iRoamer also immediately uploads all of the photos to an online gallery (presuming a WiFi connection is provided), meaning you can view the photos online within minutes of them being taken. Moreover, guests can opt to have the photo sent to an email address or text of their choosing!

LIGHTNING-FAST SETUP

Due to its lightweight nature, the Roaming Photo Booth can be set up in as little as 10 minutes, and is available for hire nationwide!

ADJUSTABLE LIGHTING

Another extremely popular feature of the Roaming Booth is its dual lighting feature. The ring light itself is made up of both white and “warm” lights, meaning the lighting and exposure can be set to perfectly match any part of your venue. This means you do not need to worry about providing any lighting fixtures.
