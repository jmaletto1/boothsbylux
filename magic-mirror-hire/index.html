Magic Mirror Hire

If you’re looking for an interactive Photo Service that goes one step further than our Classic Booths, then look no further than our Magic Mirror Hire Package. From a distance, the Mirror looks like any other mirror. But hidden behind the glass is an interactive touch screen, high-spec camera and well-powered photo software. This creates a fully interactive service that never fails to wow guests at events. They simply tap the screen and the process begins. The Mirror provides messages of encouragement between each photo, and can even allow users to sign their prints afterwards!

Our full-sized mirror takes three polaroid-standard, full-outfit catching portrait photos that are projected into any of the wide range of templates available for you to select from our vast collection.

Our Magic Mirror Service has been premiered by many leading institutions – including Gig Guide. Be sure to also consider checking out our Classic Photo Booth and Roaming Photo Booth. We service both London & Hertfordshire. Contact us today for more information.

Magic Mirror Highlights:

LIGHTNING-FAST SETUP

As the Mirror is pre-built, this means it can be set up in most venues extremely quickly. All of the electronics (minus the printer) are housed inside, meaning it only takes 5-10 minutes to set up.

FULL-LENGTH PHOTOS

Whilst traditional Photo Booths provide landscape photos, Magic Mirrors take them in a portrait format. This means you and your guests can have your full outfit in the shots!

WEDDING GUESTBOOKS

Guestbooks are the perfect addition for Weddings and Birthdays. When your guests finish taking a photo, our Booth Specialist will print an additional copy for the Guestbook. In it, guests will be encouraged to leave a message next to their photo.

BESPOKE TEMPLATE DESIGN

We are also proud to offer a fully bespoke template-design service, something not many companies are able to do. Simply inform us of the theming of your event, and any text that you would like to be present on the template (For example – Amy’s Birthday, 5th August 2020) and we will create a fully personalised design for you. Once you have reviewed the draft, we will then continue to adjust the design until it’s perfect!

TOP-NOTCH SERVICE

Once your booth is open for use, your guests will simply be blown away by our level of service. All of your guests will have full access to our extensive chest of props, double prints and the ability to visit the booth an unlimited amount of times.

ONLINE GALLERY

Like all of our Photo Booth services, your Magic Mirror comes with an Online Gallery of all of the photos your guests took on the night. If the venue has sufficient Wi-Fi capabilities, this can actually be accessed on the night of the event too!
